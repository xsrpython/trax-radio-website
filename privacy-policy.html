Privacy Policy for Trax Radio UK

Last Updated: August 2025

Introduction

Trax Radio UK ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • We do not require user registration or account creation
  • We do not track user behavior or usage patterns

Technical Information

  • Stream Metadata Only: We may access basic stream information (song titles, artist names) for display purposes

How We Use Information

Streaming Functionality

App Performance

Data Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing

  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not sell or monetize user data

Streaming Service

  • Audio streams are provided by Trax Radio UK
  • Stream metadata comes directly from the radio station
  • No personal data is involved in streaming

Data Security

Minimal Data Handling

  • We implement minimal data collection practices
  • No sensitive information is processed
  • Standard Android security measures are applied

Stream Security

  • Audio streams use standard streaming protocols
  • No personal data is transmitted during streaming
  • All connections are encrypted where possible

Your Rights

  • You can uninstall the app at any time
  • No account deletion is necessary
